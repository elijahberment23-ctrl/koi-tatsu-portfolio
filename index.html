About Our Studio

Koi Tatsu AI Studio was born from a passion for storytelling and a need for innovative solutions. Our journey began while I was illustrating a story, facing the challenge of limited resources. I discovered the incredible potential of AI tools, and what started as a personal project soon evolved into a calling.

The name "Koi Tatsu" is inspired by the Japanese myth of the koi fish, a symbol of strength, perseverance, and courage. Like the koi that courageously swims upstream against the current to become a dragon, we embrace the challenge of mastering AI to achieve exceptional results. We believe the true art lies not just in the tool, but in the skill and dedication to overcome every creative obstacle.

Here at Koi Tatsu, our mission is to bring your unique vision to life. We are committed to navigating the creative process with you, ensuring that anything is possible if you just keep swimming.
